Dense Fog Advisory issued January 23 at 1:28PM MST until January 24 at 11:00AM MST by NWS Glasgow MT

  • Event Start: 2024-01-23T13:28:00-07:00
  • Event End: 2024-01-24T11:00:00-07:00
  • Event Certainty: Likely
  • Event Severity: Moderate
Event Description

  • WHAT:Visibility one quarter mile or less at times in dense fog.
  • WHERE:Portions of northeast Montana.
  • WHEN:Until 11 AM MST Wednesday.
  • IMPACTS:Hazardous driving conditions due to low visibility.
  • ADDITIONAL DETAILS:Ice and packed snow as well as condensation from the fog will make for slick driving conditions.

Event Instructions

If driving, slow down, use your headlights, and leave plenty of distance ahead of you.
