Event Description

* WHAT...Mixed precipitation expected. Total snow accumulations up to one inch and ice accumulations around one tenth of an inch. * WHERE...Highway 56 Bull Lake Road, Highway 93 Eureka to Whitefish, Highway 2 Kalispell to Libby, and Highway 37 Eureka to Libby. * WHEN...From 8 PM this evening to 11 AM MST Tuesday. * IMPACTS...Travel could be affected. Even with air temperatures slightly above freezing, the ground is cold enough to allow for black ice on paved surfaces.

Event Instructions

Slow down and use caution while traveling. The latest road conditions for the state you are calling from can be obtained by calling 5 1 1.
